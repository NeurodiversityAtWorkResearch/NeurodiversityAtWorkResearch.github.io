Transportation

Washington, DC/Maryland/Virginia Airports used for University of Maryland Venues

  1. Baltimore/Washington International Airport - 24.4 miles from UMaryland
    Airport shuttle, Uber/Lyft, or 75 Bus
  2. Ronald Reagon Washington National Airport - 15.3 miles from UMaryland with Metro train
    Yellow Line train (toward Mt. Vernon Square) or Blue Line train (toward Largo) to Green Line train toward Greenbelt for arrival (transferring at Gallery Place/Chinatown or L'Enfant Plaza for Yellow Line origin; transferring at L'Enfant Plaza for Blue Line origin)
  3. Washington Dulles International Airport - 32.2 miles from UMaryland with Metro train
    Silver Line to Green Line train toward Greenbelt for arrival at Dulles Airport (transferring at L'Enfant Plaza)
