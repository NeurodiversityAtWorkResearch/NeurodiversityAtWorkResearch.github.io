The continued success and growth of our research community are dependent on the generous sponsorships of organizations committed to advancing Neurodiversity at Work!

We value your ongoing commitment to advancing employment opportunities and outcomes for the neurodivergent community.  Sponsoring the Neurodiversity at Work Research Conference is one more tangible way to demonstrate your commitment. Your generosity will increase opportunities for this unique neurodiversity employment community to network, collaborate, and renew.

Please review the sponsorship options below and consider sponsoring the event at a level suitable for your organization. If you are interested in sponsorship, please contact Hala at hpannabi@uw.edu.

Neurodiversity at Work

Our mission is to inspire and grow a community of researchers whose work will inform the creation of educational pathways that improve employment outcomes for neurodivergent people and transform the workplace to include and empower them.
